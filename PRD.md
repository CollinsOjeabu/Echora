### **New Product Concept: "Echora" (Enhanced)**

**Tagline:**  
*"Your AI research assistant that learns from everything you read, watch, and save—then creates authentic content from your knowledge."*

**How It Works:**
```
1. You save research (articles, videos, podcasts, PDFs)
```
